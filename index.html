ABOUT

Hi I’m William, I am a singer songwriter from Pevensey (near Eastbourne). I have had a burning passion for performance from a very early age and knew that this was the career I wanted to pursue. Coming from Eastbourne, a place where musical theatre and drama are a part of its fabric, I always found opportunities to perform - most recently playing the part of Tony in west side story.  I Absolutely love to be on stage and bring joy to people. But I have a voice that I want to share with the world and the best way I know how, is through song writing and recording my own songs. I hope you can relate and enjoy my songs as each and every one of them is from a place of truth and genuine emotion.
